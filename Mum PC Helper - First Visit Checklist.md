MUM PC HELPER — FIRST VISIT CHECKLIST


Step 1 — Access and setup
- Log into Google Drive if needed
- Open the Mum PC Helper docs
- Confirm internet is working
- Open this checklist first


Step 2 — Ask the simple opening questions
- What feels slow?
- What do you use most on this PC?
- Is anything not working properly?
- Are there files you are scared to delete?


Step 3 — Quick system check
- Check free disk space
- Look at Desktop clutter
- Check Downloads folder
- Ask about browser slowness
- Ask about printer, email, login, or pop-up issues


Step 4 — Cleanup in the right order
1. Downloads
2. Desktop
3. Recycle Bin
4. Documents only if needed
5. Pictures/Videos only if needed


Step 5 — Safe cleanup rule
- Delete only obvious junk first
- Leave anything unclear alone
- Move uncertain items into a review area rather than deleting them
- Do not touch system folders


Step 6 — Downloads pass
Look for:
- old installers
- duplicate downloads
- zip files already extracted
- junk PDFs
- random temporary files


Step 7 — Desktop pass
Look for:
- duplicate shortcuts
- files that belong in Documents or Pictures
- obvious clutter that can be organised or removed


Step 8 — Notes during the visit
Record:
- what you deleted
- what you moved
- what still needs reviewed
- any program that looks unused or suspicious


Step 9 — End of session
- Empty Recycle Bin
- Check free space again
- Test whether the PC feels faster
- Save notes
- Decide whether another pass is needed


Important
Do not try to fix everything in one visit.
The goal is safe improvement, not aggressive cleanup.
