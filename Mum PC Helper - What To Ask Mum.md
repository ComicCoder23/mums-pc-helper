MUM PC HELPER — WHAT TO ASK MUM


Ask these questions at the start so you fix the right problem instead of guessing.


1. What feels slow?
Examples:
- turning on
- opening the browser
- checking email
- finding files
- everything in general


2. What do you use this PC for most?
Examples:
- email
- Facebook
- YouTube
- documents
- photos
- shopping
- printing


3. Is anything not working properly?
Examples:
- printer
- sound
- internet
- email
- pop-ups
- login problems
- browser issues


4. Are there files you are worried about losing?
This helps avoid deleting something important.


5. Are there folders or files you do not understand?
These may need reviewed rather than deleted.


6. Do you download a lot of things and forget about them?
This helps identify whether Downloads is the main clutter area.


7. Do you know which programs you still use?
This helps later if unused apps need reviewed.


8. Have you noticed strange pop-ups, scams, or odd behaviour?
Important for deciding whether a security check is needed.


9. When did the PC start feeling worse?
This can help narrow whether the issue is gradual clutter or something newer.


10. What would make today feel like a success?
Examples:
- more space
- less clutter
- faster startup
- easier to find files
- fewer pop-ups


Good follow-up questions
- Do you still use this?
- Can this be deleted?
- Is this important?
- Have you opened this recently?
- Would you rather keep this just in case?


What to listen for
Strong clues include:
- “I don’t know what that is”
- “I never use that”
- “That keeps popping up”
- “I just click off it”
- “I can never find anything”
- “It takes ages to open”


Rule
Do not assume.
Ask first, then clean.


End goal
Understand:
- what matters to her
- what the real problem is
- what is safe to remove
- what should be left alone
